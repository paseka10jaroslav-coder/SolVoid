# SolVoid: The Digital Fortress for Solana

## Mission
SolVoid is an elite Privacy Lifecycle Management (PLM) platform for the Solana ecosystem. We bridge the gap between **passive auditing** (identifying where you are exposed) and **active defense** (cryptographically shielding your assets).

In an era of transparent blockchains, every transaction you make is a data point for MEV bots, analytics firms, and malicious actors. SolVoid provides the "Tactical Command Center" to regain control of your on-chain identity.

## Core Pillars

### 1. Privacy Passport (Scanning)
The Privacy Passport is your comprehensive on-chain health record. It aggregates data from thousands of transactions to determine your **Privacy Score**.
- **Identity Linkage**: Tracing identity from CEXs or KYC'd wallets.
- **Metadata Hygiene**: Checking for leaks in memos, logs, and account data.
- **MEV Resilience**: Analyzing vulnerability to sandwiching and front-running.
- **State Exposure**: Detecting if your identity is permanently written to third-party program states.
- **Network Anonymity**: Evaluating RPC and IP-level exposure.

### 2. Surgical Rescue (Active Defense)
Don't just watch your privacy leak—neutralize the threat. Our "Surgical Rescue" workflow allows users to:
- Identify compromised assets.
- Route them through decentralized shielding protocols.
- Cleanse the link between their public identity and their private wealth.

### 3. Shadow Vault (ZK Shielding)
The heart of our active defense is the Shadow Vault. Leveraging Zero-Knowledge Proofs (Groth16), users can deposit SOL/SPL tokens into a hidden state tree and withdraw them via anonymous relayers, breaking all on-chain links.

## Why SolVoid?
Traditional "mixers" are often uncompliant or lack transparency into *why* you need privacy. SolVoid is **clinical and authoritative**. We show you the exact transaction that leaked your data and provide the surgical tools to fix it.
